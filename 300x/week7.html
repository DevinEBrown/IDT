Reflection

The interactive module on Introduction to Web Design Layout fits within your minicourse by practically applying the concepts learned. Learners will interact with the e-learning in this module through a matching activity. This interactivity was chosen to reinforce the concepts visually and engage learners actively in the learning process.

I used Articulate 360 to create the interactive module, and I found it relatively easy to use. The user experience was intuitive, allowing me to focus more on the content and design of the module. Compared to my initial experience with H5P, which lacked an intuitive user experience, Articulate 360 was a better authoring tool for creating interactive e-learning modules.

For future projects, I may explore other alternatives, such as Adobe Captivate, to see how they compare features and ease of use. However, based on my experience with Articulate 360, I would consider using it again for its user-friendly interface and robust functionality.
