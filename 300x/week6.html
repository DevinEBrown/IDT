Reflection

The storyboard I created fits perfectly within my minicourse on web design layout. This introductory lesson lays the foundation for understanding key concepts and principles in web design. The storyboarding process was quite enlightening. It helped me visualize the flow of the lesson and ensured that the content was organized logically. I found it particularly useful for planning each scene's onscreen content, narration, and programming notes. It also allowed me to identify gaps or areas needing further elaboration. I would use storyboards again to create additional content for my minicourse. They are a valuable tool for planning and organizing educational material

For this storyboard, I used a template provided by Devlin Peck as a guide. The template was relatively easy to use, with clear sections for each scene and prompts for onscreen content, narration, and programming notes. However, I sometimes found it challenging to condense my ideas into the limited space provided for each scene.
