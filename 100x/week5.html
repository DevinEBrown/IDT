UX of EdTech

The UX of EdTech community is a platform that unites UX professionals, EdTech companies, and stakeholders in the education sector. The community aims to empower and recognize UX practitioners working on educational products and services, welcoming educators and professionals from diverse backgrounds interested in applying UX principles to education and the evolving landscape of learning and work. I have been a member of the community since 2021 and have benefited from the following:

  • Access to Industry Experts: The community facilitates connections with industry experts and thought leaders in UX and EdTech. Members can engage in discussions, seek advice, and learn from experienced professionals
  • Professional Development: provides professionals with specialized knowledge and insights related to UX design in the education sector. Members can learn about best practices, case studies, and innovative approaches to designing educational products and services on UX of EdTech's blog and Podacast
  • Job Opportunities: Members can discover job openings or freelance projects specifically related to UX design in EdTech
